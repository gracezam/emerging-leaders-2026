About the Workshop

Welcome

The 2026 Emerging Leaders Workshop: Changing the Face of Cancer Center Leadership equips developing cancer center leaders with a foundation in leadership within the specific context of matrix comprehensive cancer centers.

The workshop curriculum cultivates leadership excellence and a deep understanding of organizational structure and strategy, exploring what it means to lead within a complex, matrixed institution navigating shared authority, interdisciplinary collaboration, and institutional mission.

In addition to skill-building, the workshop offers strengths identification through the CliftonStrengths Assessment, unique networking and team-building opportunities, and concrete strategies for career advancement within NCI-designated cancer centers.

This workshop is a partnership between the University of New Mexico Comprehensive Cancer Center and USC Norris Comprehensive Cancer Center, two NCI-designated institutions committed to developing the next generation of diverse cancer center leaders.

Faculty

Featured Speakers

Caryn Lerman, PhD

Director, USC Norris Comprehensive Cancer Center Associate Dean for Cancer Programs H. Leslie and Elaine S. Hoffman Chair in Cancer Research USC Distinguished Professor, Keck School of Medicine
🏥 USC Norris Profile

Dr. Caryn Lerman is nationally recognized for her cancer prevention research bridging neuroscience, genomics, pharmacology, and behavioral science. She has published over 380 peer-reviewed articles.

An elected member of the National Academy of Medicine, Dr. Lerman has served as President of the Association of American Cancer Institutes, member of the NCI Board of Scientific Advisors, the National Human Genome Research Advisory Council, and the National Institute on Drug Abuse Advisory Council.

Dr. Lerman is the former chair of the NCI Cancer Center Review Committee, where she played a pivotal role in evaluating NCI-designated cancer centers across the country.

Yolanda Sanchez, PhD

Director and CEO, UNM Comprehensive Cancer Center The Maurice and Marguerite Liberman Distinguished Chair in Cancer Research Professor, Department of Internal Medicine
🏥 UNM Profile

Dr. Yolanda (Yoli) Sanchez is the first Hispanic/Latin woman to lead an NCI-designated Comprehensive Cancer Center. She is an international expert in DNA damage response pathways.

Prior to joining UNM, Dr. Sanchez served as Associate Director of Basic Sciences at Dartmouth Cancer Center. As Director and CEO of UNMCCC, she oversees all cancer research and clinical programs across all UNM schools, colleges, departments, and facilities.

Dr. Sanchez collaborates with UNM and New Mexico's legislative and executive leadership to bring the latest cancer diagnostics and therapeutics to New Mexicans.

Chanita Hughes Halbert, PhD

Executive Vice Chair & Vice Chair for Research; Distinguished Professor, Population and Public Health Sciences Dr. Arthur and Priscilla Ulene Chair in Women's Cancer, Keck School of Medicine Associate Director for Cancer Health Disparities, USC Norris
🏥 USC Norris Profile

Dr. Chanita Hughes Halbert is renowned for her leadership in cancer prevention and minority health research, dedicating her career to reducing disparities in cancer outcomes affecting underrepresented communities.

She has identified sociocultural, psychological, genetic, and environmental determinants of cancer health disparities and translates this knowledge into evidence-based interventions to improve health equity among racially and ethnically diverse populations.

Elected to the National Academy of Medicine in 2017, she received the American Cancer Society's Cancer Control Award in 2010 and was appointed to the NCI Board of Scientific Advisors by President Obama in 2012.

David Chow, MA, MS, PCC

Leadership Development Executive Coach Founder, Leading Together and Trusted Advisor Associates, LLC
💼 LinkedIn Profile

In 2004, David Chow founded Leading Together, providing executive coaching, leadership development, and organizational consulting. His passion is inspiring leaders toward personal mastery and psychological well-being.

A highly effective executive coach, David has coached hundreds of leaders from the C-Suite to emerging leaders across high-tech, biotech, healthcare, engineering, research/academia, government, and non-profit organizations.

David has designed and facilitated custom leadership programs worldwide on Authentic Leadership, Leading with Emotional Intelligence, Executive Presence, and Strengths-Based Leadership.
